OffScape is committed to protecting your personal information and complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and NDPR.

1. Introduction

OffScape Logistics Ltd ("OffScape") is committed to protecting the personal information of everyone who uses our platform. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, how we protect it, and what rights you have over it.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

All Users: Full name, email, phone number, password (hashed — never readable), city, optional profile photo.

Pickmans/pickmans additionally: Vehicle type, NIN, scanned KYC documents, guarantor information, bank account details.

2.2 Automatically Collected

Device type, IP address, session data. For Pickmans: real-time GPS while delivery is active and while marked online.

3. How We Use Data

Service Delivery: To process orders, match Pickmans to deliveries, calculate fees, and enable real-time tracking.

Payments: Bank details used solely to process payouts and refunds. OffScape never stores card numbers.

4. Data Sharing

We share with Paystack (payments), Cloudinary (document storage), Termii (SMS), OpenRouteService (routing), and Anthropic Claude (AI support). OffScape does not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party.

5. Data Retention

  • Order records & GPS trails — 3 years
  • KYC documents — active relationship + 2 years
  • Transaction records — 6 years
  • Support transcripts — 12 months

6. Data Security

Passwords hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12). All data encrypted in transit via TLS 1.2+. JWT tokens expire after 7 days. MFA required for admin access.

7. Your Rights

  • Right to Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Correction — Update inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion — Request account and data deletion
  • Right to Portability — Request data in machine-readable format

Contact: privacy@offscape.co. We respond within 30 days.

8. Children

OffScape is not intended for persons under 18. Minors discovered will have their accounts immediately suspended and deleted.

9. Changes

We update this policy periodically. We will notify registered users via SMS or email. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Privacy: privacy@offscape.co
